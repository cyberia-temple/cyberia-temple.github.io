Cyberia is a decentralized technology project focused on building experimental systems across Linux, blockchain, cryptography, and peer-to-peer infrastructure.

This blog documents releases, infrastructure work, and ongoing experiments.

Cyberia is not a product or a startup.
It is a working system under continuous construction.

Its goal is to explore how software, networks, and coordination can exist outside centralized control.
